ABOUT US

“Patrons of Soldiers” is apolitical, nonprofit project, initiated by volunteers. Our aim is to connect philanthropists, Charity- and Nongovernmental organizations which help veterans and their families.
Everyone can become a Patron and provide services, special offers, give favorable discounts etc. to veterans.
Make your contribution to support soldiers, their children and families!
